Syllabus

  • Space bounded computation: PSPACE, NSPACE, L, NL, reductions, completeness, complementation.
  • Polynomial Hierarchy: Quantifier alternation, completeness, Alternating Turing Machines, Oracle Machines.
  • Circuits: Conditional lower bounds (Karp-Lipton Theorem, etc.), Circuit classes.
  • Randomized Computation: BPP, RP, coRP, ZPP, Error reduction, Adelman's Theorem, Randomized reductions, Randomized space computation.
  • Interactive Proofs: Definitions, Deterministic verifier, Probablisitic verifier, Public coins vs. Private coins.
