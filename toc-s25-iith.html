Syllabus

  • Context free grammars, context free languages, parse trees, Chomsky normal form, push down automata, pumping lemma for CFLs and applications.
  • Turing machines, variants, decidable and recognizable languages, undecidability theory, undecidability of the Halting problem, many-one reductions, Riceâ€™s theorem.
